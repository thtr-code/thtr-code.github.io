Privacy Policy for Vector Notch

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Overview

Vector Notch is a macOS utility app that enhances productivity through a customizable notch experience. Features include Spotify status display, music control, macro automation, clipboard enhancements, and a live camera toggle. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what we do—and do not—collect.

Data Collection

Vector Notch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All features operate entirely on your device.

We do not collect:

  • Your identity or login credentials
  • Clipboard contents
  • Spotify account information
  • Camera footage or screenshots
  • System usage data

Spotify Integration

Vector Notch uses Spotify’s local API to display your current track and allow playback control. It is read-only and no listening data or credentials are stored or transmitted.

Macro & Automation Tools

Macros allow launching apps, tabs, or utilities like the camera. These presets are saved locally and are never uploaded or tracked.

Clipboard Features

Clipboard items are temporarily stored to allow multi-copy functionality, conversion, and sharing. All data remains on your device.

Camera Access

If you enable the camera preview widget, Vector Notch will request macOS camera permission. Camera access is used only for real-time preview and nothing is recorded, saved, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Vector Notch does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party cloud services.

Security

All operations comply with Apple’s App Sandbox to ensure your data stays secure and local.
