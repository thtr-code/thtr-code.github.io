Introduction

Patchwork is a macOS clipboard management application designed with privacy and security in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

What Data We Collect

  • Clipboard Content: Clipboard history is stored locally on your device. By default, we do not transmit your clipboard data anywhere.
  • Application Data: Metadata such as timestamps, content type, and source application may be stored locally for organizational purposes.
  • Optional iCloud Sync: If enabled, clipboard history and preferences may be stored in your personal iCloud account.

How We Use Your Data

Clipboard data is used exclusively to provide the app’s functionality — such as history management, search, categorization, and previewing.

Data Protection

- All data is stored locally on your Mac by default.
- Sensitive fields (e.g., password inputs) are automatically excluded.
- You may use Incognito Mode to temporarily disable clipboard monitoring.

Third-Party Services

Patchwork does not sell or share clipboard data with third parties. If you enable iCloud, Apple’s iCloud policies apply.

Your Choices

  • Clear or delete clipboard history at any time
  • Exclude specific apps from being monitored
  • Disable sync and use only local storage
